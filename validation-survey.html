The BEIE Framework (Bangsamoro Economic and Investment Ecosystem) groups BARMM's economy into 5 connected clusters — Foundations, Transformers, Enablers, Connectors, and Financiers — all under Moral Governance. Watch the orientation video if this is new to you.

Moral Governance means transparency, accountability, and Islamic ethics guiding how investment decisions are made — the "operating system" that determines whether all other clusters function well.

Connectors link BARMM to outside markets: trade, exports, and the BIMP-EAGA regional corridor (Brunei-Indonesia-Malaysia-Philippines East ASEAN Growth Area).
