### Respondent Instructions

> **How to Answer:** For each statement, you will rate two things:
> - **Impact (1–5):** How big of an effect does this factor have on BARMM's investment ecosystem?
>   - 1 = Very small effect | 2 = Small | 3 = Moderate | 4 = Large | 5 = Very large effect
> - **Likelihood (1–5):** How likely is this factor to continue or happen in the next 5 years (2026–2030)?
>   - 1 = Very unlikely | 2 = Unlikely | 3 = Possible | 4 = Likely | 5 = Very likely

> All questions are **optional** — answer the ones you feel confident about. Your honest input helps shape the Bangsamoro Investment Roadmap.

## Scoring Reference for Analysts

### Impact × Likelihood Priority Bands

```
Total Score = Impact (1–5) × Likelihood (1–5) = Range 1–25

Priority Bands:
  1–9   → LOW (green)      — Monitor, routine action
  10–15 → MEDIUM (blue)    — Plan intervention within 1–2 years
  16–20 → HIGH (amber)     — Urgent priority, allocate resources now
  21–25 → CRITICAL (red)   — Immediate action required, existential risk/opportunity
```

### SWOT-Specific Scoring Formulas

| Quadrant | Formula | Meaning |
|----------|---------|---------|
| **Strengths** | `RI = (Impact × Likelihood) / 5` | Resilience capacity of internal assets |
| **Weaknesses** | `Risk = Impact × Likelihood` | Internal risk exposure (scale 1–25) |
| **Opportunities** | `RI = √(Impact × Likelihood)` | Opportunity exploitation potential |
| **Threats** | `VI = (Impact² × Likelihood) / 25` | External vulnerability index |
